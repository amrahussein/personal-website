# How has my DEV started?

My journey started with a scholarship by Udacity that taught me the basics of programming.

I was introduced to computational thinking; that's how to think like the computer.

In addition to, the difference between procedural and descriptive programming. Also, the importance of abstract thinking that has to do with descriptive programming

My primary motivation, besides my passion for tech, was the influence of digital transformation I saw. The influence that can be brought by the tech.

It was a dream for me to have positive influence on the world or simply to produce a useful product with a few lines of code.

By that time I decided to have core knowledge about computer science before having to specialize.

However, I decided to have a different approach, which was to specialize in a specific area: frontend development along with learning core science and exploring new stuff as I love to solve problems.

# After graduation

Fast forward after graduation, I began to be more aware of what direction I should be going, I had to know myself deeper. That's why, I seek Coursera courses in various non-tech areas, besides any activity that would introduce me to the community and people.
